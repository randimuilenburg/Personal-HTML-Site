About the Author

I currently live in Colorado with my math-enthusiast boyfriend, our “American Dingo” named Cricket, and more cases of Dr. Pepper than I have room for. I graduated from Metropolitan State University of Denver with a BA in English and Journalism. I'm an avid reader and movie-goer who is constantly riding the high of the last good story I devoured. One day, I hope to write a novel that feels like an Ari Aster film. When I'm not writing, I'm usually reading, playing video games, going to my local movie theater, sharpening my web development skills, or taking my dog on an adventure somewhere.
